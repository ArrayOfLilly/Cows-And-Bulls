# Cows & Bulls - Installation Guide (macOS)

## What you download
You will receive a ZIP archive containing `Cows & Bulls.app`.

## Install steps
1. Download the ZIP file.
2. Double-click the ZIP to extract it.
3. Drag `Cows & Bulls.app` to your `Applications` folder (recommended).
4. First launch:
   - Right-click the app and choose `Open`.
   - Click `Open` again in the warning dialog.

## If macOS blocks the app
1. Try to open the app once (it will be blocked).
2. Open `System Settings` -> `Privacy & Security`.
3. Scroll down and click `Open Anyway` for `Cows & Bulls`.
4. Launch the app again.

## Notes
- This build is distributed outside the Mac App Store.
- Because of that, macOS may show a security warning on first launch.

## Troubleshooting
- If the app does not open, move it to `Applications` and try again.
- If you still cannot open it, restart macOS and repeat the steps above.
